Alpha Chi Omega - Delta Mu

Bid Day: Spring 2025

What is it?

Bid Day is a celebration that occurs to welcome new members into the chapter. It entails:

  • introducting new members to active sisters
  • participating in fun activities (bingo, jewerly making, etc) as a way to bond with the new members
  • making sure the new members feel included into the chapter

Details:

This event is done by the Delta Mu chapter of the Alpha Chi Omega Fraternity at the University of Massachusetts Amherst The theme for this semester is: There's No Place Like AXO.

Outfits:

The outfits for this semester's Bid Day consist of a few key parts:

  1. the provided bid day shirt
  2. light wash denim jeans
  3. red or white shoes
